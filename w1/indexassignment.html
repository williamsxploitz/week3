ABOUT US

At a Glance

MERIDIAN INTERNATIONAL COLLEGE provides a safe and inspiring environment for students to learn and grow. We achieve more than outstanding results because of our passion for learning. We strive to develop self-assured and creative thinkers and to provide an education that is truly relevant to their future. We have a wealth of success stories involving students who have gone on to become leaders in their industries as a result of our more than two decades of experience. We want to tell your child those stories. We are delighted to be classified as one of Lagos State's top ten private schools in terms of socio-academics and consultancy services, despite the fact that we are a secondary institution.



Curriculum

BECE / SSCE

Our curriculum (Nigeria and British) allows us to present students for WASCE, BECE, NECO, Cambridge Lower Secondary School (Checkpoint), and finally Cambridge IGCSE.

Cambridge IGCE

Our the years we have presented students who have scored outstanding performance in the Cambridge IGCSE examinations. We For parents interested in their children writing the IGCSE Cambridge Examination. Cambridge exams help our students present their results in other to gain admission into international Universities, and colleges, while some universities in Nigeria also accept the Cambridge O levels for admission.

International Exams

We support our students who are interested in writing international Exams for schools outside Nigeria . Exams preparation for SAT & IELST.
